Getting started with IIIF: An introductory workshop on finding, sharing, and remixing IIIF images

This introductory hands-on workshop will help you get started with IIIF. It will give you the skills to locate and re-use millions of open access images from some of the world’s greatest libraries, archives and museums. Learn how to find, compare, download, embed, and share IIIF images, and have a go at making your own virtual tours and guided viewing experiences.

No previous IIIF experience or expertise is required.
